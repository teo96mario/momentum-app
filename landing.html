FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is Momentum really free?
Yes — completely. The core habit tracker, Pomodoro timer, mood journal, streaks, achievements, and data export are all free forever. There is a Pro tier in development for advanced analytics and cloud sync, but the app is fully functional without it.
Do I need to create an account?
No. Momentum stores all your data in your browser's local storage. There's no sign-up, no email, no password. Just open the app and start. The trade-off is that your data stays on your current device unless you export it.
What is a Pomodoro timer?
The Pomodoro Technique is a time management method developed by Francesco Cirillo in the 1980s. You work in focused 25-minute intervals (called "Pomodoros"), then take a 5-minute break. After four Pomodoros, you take a longer 15–30 minute break. Studies consistently show this reduces mental fatigue and improves focus.
Does Momentum work on iPhone and Android?
Yes. Momentum is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means it runs in any modern browser on any device — iOS, Android, Mac, Windows, or Linux. On mobile, you can add it to your home screen from the browser menu to use it like a native app, including offline.
Can I use Momentum without internet?
Yes. After your first visit, Momentum's service worker caches all assets. You can track habits, run the Pomodoro timer, and log your mood completely offline. Your data syncs locally so nothing is lost between sessions.
How do I install Momentum on my phone?
On Android (Chrome): look for the "Add to Home Screen" banner or tap the three-dot menu → "Install App". On iPhone (Safari): tap the Share button → "Add to Home Screen". The app will then open like any native app, without a browser bar.
How many habits can I track?
As many as you want — there's no artificial limit. Research suggests focusing on 3–5 habits at a time for the best results, but Momentum won't stop you from tracking more.
What happens to my data if I clear my browser?
Clearing browser data or localStorage will erase your habits and history. To protect against this, use the Export Data button in Settings to download a CSV backup anytime. We're working on optional cloud sync for Pro users.
Is the source code available?
Yes — Momentum is open source. You can view, fork, or contribute at github.com/teo96mario/momentum-app.
